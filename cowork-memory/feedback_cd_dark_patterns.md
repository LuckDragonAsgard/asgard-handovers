When dealing with a CD invoice, ALWAYS itemise line items and refuse upsells before paying.

**Traps:**
- Domain Expiry Protection: $10.49/MONTH (~$126/yr) — free via auto-renew toggle.
- Domain Guard ($31.47/5yr): WHOIS privacy — useless on .au (auDA mandates public registrant info).
- Premium DNS: pointless when nameservers at Cloudflare.

**How:** Find clean registration at My Profile → Notifications. Or just register at VentraIP/Cloudflare.
