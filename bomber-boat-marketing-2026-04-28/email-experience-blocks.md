## LONG (the experience, full picture — for first-touch / cold emails / EFC pitch)

> **What's on the boat:**
> - Roll up at Cafe Riviera in Maribyrnong about an hour before bounce. Free street parking on Cumberland Drive.
> - QR pass scan, on board, free drink in your hand — beer, wine, soft drink, your call.
> - Hot finger food keeps coming. Cheap bar prices the whole way to Marvel.
> - Walk into Marvel ready to roar.
> - After the final siren the boat waits 30 minutes. Cash bar open the whole way home.
> - Win, lose or draw — best part of game day.
>
> **Fares:** Adult return $55 · Child return $25 · One-way and Marvel-only fares available.
> **Boat:** Operated with Yarra River Cruises (a fleet of vessels from 25 to 160 capacity, so we scale to demand).
> **Capacity:** 60 seats per sailing for first home games — we'll add boats once we sell out.
